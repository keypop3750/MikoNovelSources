Chapter 1983: Chapter 1983: Sense of Crisis!

"That kid’s speed is downright terrifying!"

"Yeah! Looks even a bit faster than an average Great Supreme Realm Eighth Turn!"

"But I actually think what he’s best at is his insane experience and how he controls the rhythm of the fight!"

"Now that you mention it, it really is like that. Every time this kid just happens to dodge those two’s attacks—never early, never late, no more, no less!"

"This kid’s combat experience is way too rich, totally not something a young guy should have!"

The ones with better perception all started discussing, all shocked by Su Chen’s astonishing speed—especially that battle experience of his that even they found amazing!

"Still, with many people he can stay all calm and collected, but once the numbers thin out, I bet in the end this kid still won’t escape being blasted off the stage!"

At this moment, someone sighed and said.

The others heard this and all nodded in agreement!

In the end, what you compare is still strength!

They didn’t believe that young man really had the strength to face those two from Duanhai Sect!

Besides, if he really had the strength, why would he keep dodging instead of facing them head-on?!

Even that Divine Sense Disturbance of his that shocked everyone might not actually be that effective against these two!

After all, once you reach Late Stage Great Supreme Realm, every Minor Realm you break through boosts you in all aspects—including your spiritual power!

...

Of course, this was also exactly what those two from Duanhai Sect were thinking!

To be honest, they weren’t really wrong to think this way. If Su Chen’s spiritual power was still the same as before, a bit over level eighty, he really might not be able to do much to them!

As one contestant after another was blasted off the platform, on the Douwu Platform where Su Chen was, aside from Yan Feiyu, the only ones left were Su Chen, the two from Duanhai Sect, and one Great Supreme Realm Seventh Turn!

That Great Supreme Realm Seventh Turn was completely dumbfounded at this point!

First of all, nobody dared provoke Yan Feiyu; right now he was standing there with his arms crossed, watching the show!

The two from Duanhai Sect were glaring at that young man like he was their father’s killer!

And him... nobody was even paying attention to him!

What was he supposed to do?!

He knew his own strength. Other than that young man, whom he might actually be able to beat, he was no match for the other three!

But could he fight Duanhai Sect over a target?!

Even if he snatched the kill, those three available slots still wouldn’t end up in his hands!

Just as he hesitated, one of the Duanhai Sect men glanced at him and said:

"Let’s see how you dodge now!"

They felt really satisfied; just now they’d been suffocated to death by frustration!

Now, they could finally fight to their heart’s content!

"Fine, since that’s how you want it, I’ll play with you then."

Su Chen said calmly.

"Hmph, you don’t know you’re already dead!"

Hearing such arrogant words from Su Chen, the two of them felt their rage flare up all over again!

"You should be thankful the Great Court Trial forbids killing!"

One of them spat out coldly.

Hearing this, Su Chen’s lips curled into a faint smile:

"That’s exactly what I wanted to say to you."

"Stop wasting breath on him!"

The other Duanhai Sect man barked. Talking to this brat was enough to drive a man mad!

Too arrogant!

So, the two of them moved at the same time!

In that instant, their auras surged skyward, a wild storm whipped across the entire Douwu Platform, their presence rolling out in overwhelming waves!

And it didn’t stop there. Very soon, their auras hit the absolute peak, vast and majestic!

Just that aura alone had the people from the various Sects nodding in appreciation!

And right at that moment, the two truly struck!

Each of them slapped a palm forward, looking very much like two ghosts slapping a door!

As they moved, the air detonated crazily, the shrill screeching almost tearing eardrums apart. The two were like great beasts pouncing forward, their domineering, scorching presence crashing down in a mad wave!

They were bona fide Great Supreme Realm Eighth Turn experts; facing a Divine Sea Realm Sixth Turn, they couldn’t even be bothered to use a Weapon!

Because they felt it would be embarrassing, even if this brat was a bit unusual!

In the blink of an eye, the two were already very close to Su Chen!

But what confused them was that under such pressure, that kid was still standing there just fine!

And on his face, there even seemed to be a faint, almost invisible smile!

It was eerie!

Hmph, putting on a spooky act!

The two snorted coldly, shaking off the faint sense of unease that had just risen in their hearts!

Either way, he was about to die!

However, right then, the two of them suddenly froze!

Because that figure in front of them abruptly vanished from where he’d been!

It happened too fast; even with their perception, they could only catch a blurred Shadow!

At that moment, one of them suddenly felt an Energy Wave behind him, and a chill shot from his head to his toes!

A massive sense of danger engulfed his entire body!

In a flash, he turned his head—but what greeted him was a fist!

"Pffft!"

He spat out a mouthful of fresh blood, his body shooting backwards like a kite with its string cut!

The other man of course sensed it; his first reaction wasn’t to strike at Su Chen, but to instinctively pull back and open up some distance!

And all of this happened in just an instant!

Everyone’s eyes went wide!

Two Great Supreme Realm Eighth Turn experts actually failed to take down this kid—instead, one of them was taken out by him?!

This... was way too unbelievable, wasn’t it?!

But what shocked everyone even more was the speed that young man had shown in that instant!

That absolutely reached Great Supreme Realm Ninth Turn, maybe even Ninth Turn Middle Stage!

The remaining man was still shaken. If the other party’s target just now had been him, he’d have had a hard time getting out of it too!

All of a sudden, a long spear appeared in his hand. The shaft was pitch-black, gleaming with a biting cold light—it was a King Artifact!

In the next moment, his aura surged wildly, flooding crazily into his arm, then pouring into that long spear!

"Dragon Soars into the Sea!"

He let out a loud shout, raising the spear as his wrist spun over a hundred times in the span of a single breath!

Beams of spear light gathered at the spear tip, and the weapon let out a low, buzzing hum, as if filled with hunger!

Many Sect disciples close by already felt an immense pressure—the aura was too overpowering!

That Elder from Duanhai Sect revealed a gratified look as well. "Dragon Soars Across the Four Seas" was one of Duanhai Sect’s ultimate techniques, and this thrust had already grasped most of its essence!

The man suddenly lifted his head, sharp light blazing in his eyes as his tyrannical aura erupted to the limit, his wrist trembling even faster!

"Die!"

With that thunderous shout, the spear seemed to pierce through everything as it shot toward Su Chen with lightning speed!

Where the spear light passed, the void collapsed!

That fierce momentum was enough to make one’s heart tremble!

The reason he used a spear was that he was somewhat wary of the opponent’s speed; among Weapons, a spear had the greatest reach!

Of course, more importantly, this Spear Technique was the one he was most proud of!

This thrust could be said to push his strength to the absolute limit!
